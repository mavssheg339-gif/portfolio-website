About MAVSBS

Empowering Tribal Children Through Education & Values

Maharashtra Adivasi Bahu Uddeshiya Vikas Sanstha (MAVSBS) was founded in 2008 in Shegaon, Buldhana district. Our core mission is to bridge the educational gap in tribal communities by running Bal Sanskar Kendras — evening learning centers where children receive academic support, moral guidance, and cultural pride.

We believe tribal children deserve not only literacy, but the values, confidence, and character to thrive in modern society while honoring their roots.

Vision 2031

Reaching Every Tribal Child in Maharashtra

By 2031, MAVSBS envisions a network of 40 Bal Sanskar Kendras across Maharashtra, serving 1,200+ tribal children with quality value education every year.
