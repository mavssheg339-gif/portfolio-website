About MAVSBS

Maharashtra Adivasi Bahu Uddeshiya Vikas Sanstha operates structured Bal Sanskar Kendras across tribal communities in Buldhana district. The model focuses on foundational literacy, value education, hygiene awareness, and measurable academic progress.
